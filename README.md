# 🎁 FNP Sales Analysis Dashboard (Excel)

## 📌 Project Title
FNP Sales Analysis Dashboard | Microsoft Excel

---

## 📖 Short Description / Purpose

This project analyzes the sales performance of Ferns N Petals (FNP) using Microsoft Excel. The interactive dashboard provides insights into revenue, customer spending, product performance, order trends, and delivery efficiency. It helps identify top-performing occasions, categories, cities, and products to support better business decisions.

---

- Pivot Tables

- Excel Formulas

- Dashboard Design & Data Visualization

---

## 📂 Data Source

- FNP Sales Dataset
- Customer Details

- Order & Delivery Data

---

## ✨ Features / Highlights

### • Business Problem

The business wanted to understand sales performance across different occasions, product categories, months, cities, and customer purchasing behavior to improve sales strategies and inventory planning.

### • Goal of the Dashboard

To build an interactive Excel dashboard that enables users to:
- Monitor overall sales performance.
- Analyze customer purchasing patterns.
- Identify top-selling products and categories.
- Compare revenue across occasions and months.
- Evaluate delivery performance.
- Track regional sales trends.

---

## 📊 Key Business Questions

- What is the total revenue generated?
- What is the average customer spending?
- How many customers placed orders?
- Which occasion generates the highest revenue?
- Which product category performs the best?
- What are the Top 5 revenue-generating products?
- Which months generate the highest sales?
- Which cities receive the most orders?
- Which day of the week records the highest revenue?
- What is the average difference between order date and delivery date?

---

## 📈 Business Insights

- Total Revenue generated is **₹35.2 Lakhs** from **1000 customers**.
- Customers spend an average of **₹3,521 per order**, indicating healthy order values.
- **Anniversary** and **Raksha Bandhan** contribute the highest revenue among all occasions.
- **Colors**, **Soft Toys**, and **Sweets** are the top-performing product categories.
- **Magnam Set** is the highest revenue-generating product, followed closely by **Quia Gift** and **Dolores Gift**.
- Sales peak during **February** and **August**, indicating strong seasonal demand.
- **Imphal**, **Dhanbad**, and **Kavali** record the highest number of customer orders.
- **Saturday** generates the highest weekly revenue, suggesting stronger weekend sales.
- The average order-to-delivery time is approximately **5.5 days**, providing a benchmark for delivery performance.
